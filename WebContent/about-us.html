About Us

Choose Your Taste is a website all about food . Choose Your Taste was inspired by the thought of providing menu of every restaurant in a location and know the prices of items in that restaurant . A single window for knowing the best items of a particular restaurant.

A website where you can see the feedback of every restaurant given by the user so that you can choose the best restaurant and best items you can have.
